Who We Are

The Kendriya Vidyalaya Puri Alumni Association exists to help you stay connected to KV Puri, and to each other, wherever you are in your life now. Whatever you're seeking, be it KV Puri events and connections in your hometown, an excuse to come back to the Farm for Alumni meets, a job lead or a mind-expanding magazine, we've got you covered.

Benefits And Services

Career Counseling

Special forum for all the students to participate in the career counseling programme.

CV Enhancement

We help students for improvising their CV according to the current trend.

One to One Appointment

Students already pursuing 10th or 12th currently in school can book appointment for one to one career advice.

Alumni Card

All the alumni members will provided with an alumni card which is having its own significants.

Events

Frequent events are conducted by the association to keep a good environment and relations with the students.

Job Portal

We are going to introduce a job section in our allumni portal, where alumni members will post jobs and students can apply for the relevant jobs.

Yoga Advice

Special yoga practice are conducted by the society through expert yoga trainers.
