Who We Are

Founded in 2012 , the KV Puri Alumni Association aims to deliver the most effective alumni relations program anywhere in higher education. Its diverse offerings include a bimonthly events; online communication and networking tools; academic and social programming (on and off-campus); a worldwide travel/study program; and numerous Kv Puri-related products and initiatives.

Our Mission

  • The KV Puri Alumni Association seeks to reach, serve and engage all KV Puri alumni and students; to foster a lifelong intellectual and emotional connection between the School and its Students; and to provide the School with goodwill and support.
